Create a Delicious Homemade Pizza at home

create this delicious pizza with cheap ingredients

No pizza oven? No problem! Make your homemade pizza on the grill for that classic smoky, wood fire oven flavor. This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Of course, you can use whatever toppings you like!

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. Create the pizza dough by putting all the dough ingredients to a big bowl
  3. after the pizza dough is done, knead until smooth and give toppings
